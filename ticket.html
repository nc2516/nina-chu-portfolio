Airplane Ticket Redesign

The original plane ticket design is hardly designed at all. All the information is just printed on and it is quite difficult to find the important information. It takes a few seconds to find the gate, when it should not take any time at all. And from afar, it is just a jumble of letters. Using Adobe Illustrator, I redesigned the ticket for design fundamentals.

The new design is easier to read, and easier to navigate for an easy experience at the airport.
